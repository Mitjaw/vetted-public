Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026

Draft — review by a privacy-competent lawyer before launch. GDPR specifics around lawful basis, retention periods, and data-processor agreements (Stripe, Resend, Anthropic) need to be locked down.

1. Who we are

The data controller for personal information processed by Vetted is [Mitja Wilms, full address]. Contact: privacy@vetted.wilmstech.com.

2. What we collect

  • Account data: email, password hash, signup timestamp, last visit.
  • Subscription data: tier, period end, Stripe customer + subscription IDs (we do not store card numbers — Stripe does).
  • Usage logs: IP address, request paths, response codes. Retained 30 days for security and rate-limiting.
  • Analytics: we do not run third-party tracking by default. If we add product analytics later, we will update this policy and ask for opt-in consent.

3. What we do not collect

We do not collect biometric data, location data, or anything beyond what's needed to run the service. We do not sell or share your data with advertisers.

4. Channel and stock data

Vetted analyses publicly accessible YouTube videos via TranscriptAPI and stock prices from yfinance / Tiingo. This data is not personal data about you.

5. Lawful basis

We process account, subscription, and usage data on the basis of contract performance (Art 6(1)(b) GDPR) — we need the data to provide the service you signed up for. Email delivery uses legitimate interests (Art 6(1)(f)) for transactional messages, and explicit consent for any non-essential email.

6. Sub-processors

  • Stripe (Ireland) — payment processing and Customer Portal.
  • Resend (USA, EU data region available) — transactional email delivery.
  • Hetzner (Germany) — hosting infrastructure.
  • Anthropic (USA) — Claude API for stock-mention extraction. Only public YouTube transcripts are sent — never your personal data.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

You can request access, correction, deletion, portability, and restriction of your personal data, and you can object to processing. Contact privacy@vetted.wilmstech.com; we'll reply within 30 days. You may also lodge a complaint with the data-protection authority of [your federal state] (BfDI for federal-level matters).

8. Retention

Account data: until you delete the account, plus a 30-day grace period. Subscription/billing records: 10 years (German tax law, §147 AO). Usage logs: 30 days.

9. Cookies

We set one cookie: a session cookie used to keep you logged in. It's HTTP-only, SameSite=Lax, and contains an opaque session identifier. No advertising or tracking cookies.

10. Changes

We will notify active subscribers by email at least 14 days before any material change to this policy.
